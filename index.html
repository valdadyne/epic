The Nice Guys

The Nice Guys is a 2016 American action buddy noir comedy film directed by Shane Black and written by Anthony Bagarozzi and Black. The film stars Russell Crowe, Ryan Gosling, Angourie Rice, Matt Bomer, Margaret Qualley, Keith David and Kim Basinger and follows a pair of 1977 Los Angeles private eyes who investigate a missing girl.

Premier Date: May 15, 2016

Location:Prestige Plaza
